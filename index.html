Services We Offer

Services

We Offer Variety of Dental Services.

Non-Surgical Root Canal

The non-surgical Root canal treatment is often a straightforward procedure. We remove the infected pulp on the inside of the tooth and then carefully clean and disinfect, then fill and seal.

Retreatment

Sometimes, a tooth that has been treated doesn't heal properly and can become painful or diseased months or even years after treatment. This treatment is a second chance to save your tooth.

Cracked Tooth

As an Endodontists we specialize in saving cracked teeth and will cater treatment to the type, location, and extent of the crack. The sooner your tooth is treated, the better the outcome.

Surgical Root Canal

Sometimes the infection is too significant or source of pain is not visible in the x-ray. That's when a surgical root canal (or Apicoectomy) is required to access the root canal through the gum and remove the infected tissue.
