Services We Offer

Services

We Offer Variety of Dental Services.

Root Canal

treat a tooth with infected or damaged pulp, which is the soft tissue inside containing nerves and blood vessels.

Apicoectomy

surgical procedure to remove the root tip of a tooth to treat an infection that cannot be resolved with a standard root canal.

Internal Bleaching

dental procedure used to whiten a non-vital tooth that has become discolored from the inside, typically after a root canal treatment or due to trauma.
